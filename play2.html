An Ideal Husband

Act 1 - _The Octagon Room in Sir Robert Chiltern’s House in Grosvenor Square_.

MASON

[_Announcing guests from the top of the staircase_.]

Mr. and Lady Jane Barford. Lord Caversham.

LORD CAVERSHAM

Good evening, Lady Chiltern! Has my good-for-nothing young son been here?

LADY CHILTERN

[_Smiling_.]

I don’t think Lord Goring has arrived yet.

MABEL CHILTERN

[_Coming up to_ LORD CAVERSHAM.]

Why do you call Lord Goring good-for-nothing?
